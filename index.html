Free Appliance Pickup Service

Reliable Appliance Pickup provides free appliance pickup for refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, freezers, dishwashers, and other household appliances.

We focus on reusing working appliances and helping people remove unwanted appliances in a simple and reliable way.

Free Pickup Requirements

To qualify for free pickup:

  • Appliances must be fully working and in good condition.
  • We also consider free pickup when at least 80% of the appliances collected are in working condition.
  • Appliances should be accessible and ready for pickup.

We provide free pickup within 25 miles of our major service areas. For locations outside our normal service area, a small travel fee may apply to help cover additional fuel and transportation expenses.
