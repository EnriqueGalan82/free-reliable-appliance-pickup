Free Appliance Pickup Service

Reliable Appliance Pickup provides free pickup for refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, freezers, dishwashers, and other household appliances.

We focus on reusing working appliances and helping people remove unwanted appliances in a simple and reliable way.

Why Choose Reliable Appliance Pickup?

  • Free pickup for qualifying working appliances
  • Fast and reliable scheduling
  • Helping reuse appliances instead of sending them to waste
  • Serving homeowners, landlords, and businesses

Free Pickup Requirements

  • Appliances must be fully working and in good condition.
  • We also consider pickup when at least 80% of collected appliances are working.
  • Appliances should be accessible and ready for pickup.

We provide free pickup within 25 miles of our major service areas. Locations outside our normal service area may require a small travel fee for additional fuel and transportation costs.

Appliances We Pick Up

  • Refrigerators
  • Washers / Washing Machines
  • Dryers
  • Stoves & Ranges
  • Dishwashers
  • Freezers
  • Multiple Appliances

How It Works

  1. Submit your pickup request.
  2. Provide appliance details.
  3. We confirm if it qualifies.
  4. We schedule your pickup.
